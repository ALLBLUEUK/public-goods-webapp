Night Market Entry Game

夜市摆摊挑战 Night Market Entry Game

你们都在考虑今晚要不要去夜市摆摊。进场的人越多，每个摊位分到的顾客就越少。
Each of you is deciding whether to enter the night market tonight. The more stalls enter, the fewer customers each stall gets.

如果你进场，还要决定摆几个小时。人少时多摆更赚钱，人太多时多摆反而可能亏钱。
If you enter, you must also choose how many hours to stay open. When few stalls enter, longer hours can pay off. When too many enter, longer hours can actually lose money.
