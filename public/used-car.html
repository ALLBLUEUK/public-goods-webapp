规则 Rules

中文

买家先匿名报价，报价随机送给卖家。卖家知道自己的车质量，看到报价后决定卖或不卖。

如果成交，系统揭示质量并结算。买家得分 = 车辆价值 - 成交价；卖家得分 = 成交价。

如果不成交，买家得 0；卖家保留车辆，得自己的保留价值。

English

Buyers bid first and bids are randomly sent to sellers. Sellers know their own quality and decide whether to sell after seeing the bid.

If trade happens, the quality is revealed and payoffs are settled. Buyer payoff = car value - price; seller payoff = price.

If there is no trade, the buyer gets 0 and the seller keeps the car at the seller's keep value.
