Run multiple classroom games on one online site. Choose a game first, then open the teacher or student view.

Game 1

公共品博弈 Public Goods Game

适合讲公共品、搭便车、个人激励与集体激励之间的张力。
Good for public goods, free-riding, and the tension between private and collective incentives.

Game 2

最后通牒博弈 Ultimatum Game

适合讲公平偏好、低报价拒绝，以及“效用不只等于货币”。
Good for fairness preferences, rejection of low offers, and the idea that utility is not just money.

Game 3

二手车市场 Used Car Market

适合讲信息不对称、逆向选择、预期质量变化与愿付价格下降。
Good for asymmetric information, adverse selection, and falling willingness to pay when expected quality declines.

Game 4

奶茶店爆单挑战 Milk Tea Rush

适合讲协调失败、瓶颈工位和“为什么别人慢时，我一个人冲太快反而不划算”。
Good for coordination failure, bottlenecks, and why overexerting alone can backfire when someone else is slow.

Game 5

夜市摆摊挑战 Night Market Entry Game

适合讲进入决策、拥挤效应，以及“人一多就不一定值得继续干更久”的市场过程。
Good for entry decisions, crowding effects, and the market process in which staying longer is not always worth it when too many competitors enter.
