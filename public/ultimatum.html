规则 Rules

中文

每轮总额是 10。提出者先决定给对方多少；回应者看到报价后，只能选择接受或拒绝。

接受：提出者得 10 - 报价，回应者得报价。拒绝：双方都得 0。

前半阶段角色固定，后半阶段整体交换。每轮重新随机匹配，但保持匿名。

English

Each round uses a pie of 10. Proposers decide an offer first; responders then accept or reject after seeing the offer.

If accepted, proposer payoff = 10 - offer and responder payoff = offer. If rejected, both get 0.

Roles stay fixed in Stage 1 and switch in Stage 2. Matching is random and anonymous every round.
