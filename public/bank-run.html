Campus Bank Rumor

校园银行传言 Campus Bank Rumor

你们 6 个人都把钱存在同一家校园银行里。如果银行撑到最后一天，留在里面的人可以拿到更高回报。
All six of you deposited money in the same campus bank. If the bank survives to the last day, those who stay earn the higher return.

但每个人都会私下听到一条传言。真正难的不是算术，而是猜别人会不会因为传言先跑去取钱。
But each player privately hears a rumor. The real challenge is not arithmetic. It is guessing whether other people will panic and run first.
